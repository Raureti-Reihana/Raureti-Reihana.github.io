Learning Plan - Bootcamp

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

I would like to get a job in web development and be part of an environment that helps me to continue to learn and widen my programming knowledge and skills. I want to have the flexibility to work remotely and have better work life balance. I would also like to be able to teach these skills to my iwi in the future in hopes of helping those interested move into the tech industry.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

Looking at my previous answers from sprint-2, nothing has changed. My strengths lie in my curiosity and perseverance. My limitations for learning would mainly be time. I have a responsibility to take care and provide for my family in all aspects and is one of my highest priorities and this can take a lot of my time. At the same time my family is one of my biggest motivations to push through hard times and persevere.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I think my biggest challenges will be keeping up with the work. I have been managing to keep up with the work so far but I am needing all of my spare time that I have and to think bootcamp is going to be 1.5x the amount of work now is a bit daunting. I think having to talk in front of people more often could be challenging but I am willing to take these challenges on.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I think I do quite well with my time management but with the extra workload I think I may need to tighten up my time management even more or even work less hours at work. I would also like to work on my communicating skills with other students.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I expect that the bootcamp team is there to support us just like they have for the start of this course. The team is very quick to reply and really makes it easy for us to stay on track without losing focus on the current activity.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

I will continue to ask for help in the discord and I will also be using chat gpt to assist me to understand concepts and methods. I commit to setting aside one day per week where I will meet with other people that are in my team when we are pair programming, preferably during the weekend so we can discuss, plan and work together. I commit to being open minded and accepting of others work styles and do my best to adapt and work cohesively. We can do this by discussing and planning every time before we start working.
