What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My long term goal is to be able to work remotely and have the skills to contribute to projects for my iwi and teach others from my iwi about coding.
In between now and then I would love to be in a workplace that allows me to learn while working.

Description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

I would say my strengths lie in my curiosity and perseverance. If i am curious and interested in what i am learning then i can continue to keep diving into learning without getting tired.
My limitations for learning would mainly be time. I have a responsibility to take care and provide for my family in all aspects and is one of my highest priorities and this can take a lot of my time. At the same time my family is one of my biggest motivations to push through hard times and persevere.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to develop my time management skills, so I can efficiently finish my work without the stress of deadlines.
Since starting Dev Academy I have noticed the huge value in these types of skills and have seen the positive effect these skills have had on my work ethics.
I would also like to be more of a contributor when it comes to working with other people
